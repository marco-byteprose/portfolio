I've worn many hats and proven I'm able to learn efficiently to be able to deliver business value.

This endeavor marks a pivotal step towards aligning my career path with my inherent passion for programming.

My expertise encompasses a wide array of skills crucial for success in diverse professional environments. I possess strong leadership, communication, and team-building abilities, honed through years of experience in customer relations and technical support roles. With a background in sales, I excel in identifying and meeting customer needs. Additionally, I have a keen interest in research, data analytics, and software programming. My proficiency extends to technical documentation, hardware/software troubleshooting, training, and education, bolstered by project management skills and a knack for analytical problem-solving. I thrive in fostering cross-functional collaboration to achieve organizational goals and provide business value.
